WHO WE ARE

Here at "TFG" we provide digital copies of original hand painted flash sheets, available to be bought as part of our official gallery releases.

Housed artists partner with us to create exclusive series of flash sheets, hand painted specifically for their "Flash Gallery Collection".

WHAT WE DO

Each artists series contains 100 digital copies of 10 exclusive flash designs per artist, for you to buy and own.

Each series released will become a part of the TFG's Collections and can only be purchased as digital prints.

WHY

Tattooing is one of the worlds eldest and most sacred artforms therefore always deserves to be at the forefront of any artistic platform.

Here at TFG or focus is to provide exclusive collections of hand drawn art, for you to own on the digital platform.

COMMUNITY

Join our discord and become part of a tattoo/arts community where you can have non-stop discussions about tattooing, tattoo history, tattoo ideas, painting, exhibitions, with various artists and collectors from all around the globe.

Your flash will also give you exclsuive benefits as holders such as gallery raffles, exhibitions entry and new releases and more.
